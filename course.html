Course details

When completing the course, you will be able to enjoy the silky movement of the Offhand arm, and easily complete common flourishing works (flowers, birds, lace decorations, etc.). You can master the basic elements and matching rules of OF, and perform classical graphic design combining text and painting. By being familiar with the history of Offhand Flourishing and the works of masters of

Schedule:One lesson per week, 24 lessons in total. The main content revolves around basic skills and techniques, and is interspersed with content such as appreciation of master works and styles, aesthetic awareness and cognition every month. The course content is rich and there are a lot of homework exercises.

Meet the Expert Behind Our Course

Epiphany_penman@Instagram

A calligraphy expert and enthusiast.

Together with Nibway, a top senior figure in the field, he completed the Chinese translation and annotation of "Lessons in Offhand Flourishing" by William E. Dennis, a master of the golden age. He also received the support of Dr. Vitolo, an authoritative person from the American IAMPETH organization, and wrote the preface for the Chinese translation and annotation.

In 2020, he was invited to participate in the "Graceful-The Art of Letters" International English Calligraphy Exhibition in Singapore.
